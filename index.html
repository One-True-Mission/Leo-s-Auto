Welcome

Leo's Automotive has been the preferred auto repair shop in Kyle and Buda for over 13 years. Founded in 2010 as a family-owned business, we've built our reputation one honest repair at a time — treating every customer and every vehicle with the same commitment to quality and care.

Our ASE Certified Mechanics bring expertise to every job, using name brand parts and delivering a nationwide "Peace of Mind" warranty on all services. Whether it's a quick oil change or a major engine repair, we do it right and we stand behind our work.

Short on time? Drop your car off and hop in our free shuttle. Same-day turnaround is our goal on every service. Walk-ins are always welcome — no appointment needed.

📋
State Inspections

Fast, reliable Texas state vehicle inspections. Walk in anytime — no appointment needed. We get you in and out quickly so you can move on with your day.

🔧
Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, filter replacements, fluid flushes, and multi-point inspections following manufacturer-recommended intervals to keep your vehicle running strong.

⚙️
Engine Repair

From check engine diagnostics to full engine overhauls, our ASE Certified technicians handle complex repairs on both foreign and domestic vehicles.

🔄
Transmission Service

Transmission fluid services, diagnostics, and repairs to keep your drivetrain operating the way it was designed to — smoothly and reliably.

🛑
Brakes & Calipers

Complete brake system service including pad and rotor replacement, caliper work, brake fluid flushes, and ABS diagnostics. Safety is always the priority.

❄️
A/C Repair & Service

Full A/C system diagnostics, refrigerant recharge, leak detection, and component repair. Stay cool no matter how hot a Texas summer gets.

🚗
Wheel Alignments

Precision alignment to restore proper steering response, reduce uneven tire wear, and improve fuel efficiency — all in a fast, same-day turnaround.

🔍
Diagnostics

Modern diagnostic tools to accurately read fault codes and identify problems across all vehicle systems. We find the root cause — no guesswork, no wasted money.

🔩
General Repair

Starters, alternators, belts, hoses, suspension components, cooling system repairs and more. All makes and models, all backed by our nationwide warranty.
